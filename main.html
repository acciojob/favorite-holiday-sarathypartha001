  1. Uluwatu Temple: A breathtaking cliff-side temple overlooking the Indian Ocean.
  2. Tegallalang Rice Terraces: Famous for their stunning views and traditional irrigation systems.
  3. Mount Batur: A scenic volcano, popular for sunrise hikes and panoramic views.
  4. Ubud Monkey Forest: A nature reserve and temple complex home to hundreds of playful monkeys.
  5. Seminyak Beach: Known for its vibrant nightlife, luxury resorts, and stunning sunsets.
  • Nasi Goreng: Indonesia's famous fried rice dish with chicken, prawns, and a fried egg on top.
  • Sate Lilit: Grilled skewers of spiced minced meat, usually served with rice and peanut sauce.
  • Babi Guling: A traditional Balinese roasted pork dish.
  • Lawar: A mixed vegetable and meat dish, often served with coconut and spices.
  • Pisang Goreng: Deep-fried bananas, a popular local dessert and snack.
