Paris, France

  2. Louvre Museum: The world’s largest art museum.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A stunning example of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica: Situated at the highest point in Paris.
  5. Champs-Élysées: A world-famous avenue filled with shops.
